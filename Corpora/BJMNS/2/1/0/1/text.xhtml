Nat Gets Attacked by Ele the Alien Under the Sea

Dad can I jump off that cliff over there?  I begged.

Of course Nat, my dad told me.

Oh, let me introduce myself.  My name is Nat Shoemen.  I'm eight years old. I am a boy and have dark brown eyes. My hair is pitch black and I have dark skin.  I weigh about 52.5 pounds and am four feet three inches tall. I love cliff diving into the ocean.  You cliff dive by going to the top of a cliff.  Then when you get to the top you dive into the water. Well, there are some things about me.

Right now we are on vacation and at a cliff diving area.  The cliff diving area is over the Pacific Ocean. Now back to the story.  I went over to a cliff that was 300 feet high and I did a triple back flip off the edge and into the ocean.

 Sploosh!!!

I landed in the murky water.  All of a sudden some seaweed wrapped around my leg.

I looked down and stared at the evilest alien I had ever seen (I had only seen two before) yanking down on my leg.  She had pea green eyes, dark brown hair, freckles, and was three feet tall. She was wearing a green shirt, black pants and black boots. She pulled and tugged me further down under the ocean into a giant bubble. I finally let out my breath which I had been holding all of this time. I gazed around inside the bubble and was amazed at how elegant her home was.

Dad, I found a human the alien told another alien, I think it was her father.

Good work, Ele the alien, who I think was the father, said. Now we can start asking it questions.

I found out they were planning to invade Earth.

I'm going to have to warn people I said to myself. But, how?

I knew the surface was too far under the ocean to swim to the top.  I observed a dolphin through the wall of the bubble. If I could break through the wall of the bubble I could ride the dolphin to the surface. I said to myself. I quickly exploded through the wall of the bubble and onto the dolphin as he raced by and he immediately swam to the surface near the shore.  I was out of breath, but I made it.

  Now, I needed to find a way to warn people of the planned invasion by aliens.

I told my dad all that had just happened to me.

Nat, there are no such thing as aliens he explained to me.

I don't know what happened to the aliens, but I am never going cliff diving again. I am sure of that.
